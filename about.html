Why "Kinch"?

Kinch is the nickname of Stephen Dedalus in James Joyce's Ulysses. He's a writer and artist who connects the infinite to the infinitely small. He's also a stand-in for Joyce who is doing the same thing with Ulysses itself. Kinch is shorthand for the knife's edge of language that cuts through it all.

It also seems not to be a swear in any common language worldwide, and ought to be pretty good for SEO.
