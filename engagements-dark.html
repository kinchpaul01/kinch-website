How I work with clients

Every organization's situation is different. Here's how I can help.

Strategy Consulting

For executives who need a clear, actionable AI and data strategy—one that connects directly to business outcomes, not just a technology wishlist.

I work with leadership teams to assess where they are, identify the highest-value opportunities, and build a practical roadmap for getting there.

  • Assess your current data assets, capabilities, and gaps
  • Identify where AI and data can drive the most competitive value
  • Build a prioritized roadmap tied to business outcomes
  • Define what success looks like and how to measure it

What you get: A clear strategy and roadmap that your leadership team can act on—with realistic timelines, resource requirements, and expected outcomes.

Training & Workshops

For leadership teams that need to get smart about AI and data—fast. These sessions are practical and decision-oriented, not technical deep-dives.

Participants leave with a clear understanding of what the technology can do, where its real limits are, and how to make better decisions about it.

  • AI fundamentals for business leaders—what it is, what it isn't, and what it means for your industry
  • Data strategy essentials—how to think about data as a competitive asset
  • Evaluating AI investments—frameworks for making smart bets
  • Custom workshops tailored to your team's specific challenges

What you get: A leadership team that can have informed conversations about AI and data, ask the right questions, and make confident decisions.
